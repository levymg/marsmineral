Pelletizing Technology

Mars Mineral is the leader in agitation agglomeration - the conversion of fine powders and dusts into spherical pellets. We've provided equipment, services and single-source systems for the steel, foundry, chemical, mining, cement, lime and fertilizer industries, and consumer products manufacturers.

For more than 40+ years, worldwide customers have relied upon our expertise to identify the application, supply equipment that has been verified by testing and on-site trials, and provide and install complete turnkey agitation pelletizing systems. Our equipment and services are backed by solid experience, an engineering staff qualified in material handling, a veteran technical support group and in-house manufacturing expertise.

Whether your application calls for disposal, recycling or producing consumer or industrial products, we have the experience to work with you to provide the equipment or system to meet your needs.
