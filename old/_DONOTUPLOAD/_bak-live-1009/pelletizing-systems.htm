Systems

Mars Mineral has the capability of supplying to you a single pelletizer or a complete pelletizing system, according to your specific requirements.

Our experienced personnel will design, engineer, fabricate and furnish a system that can include material preparation, mixing, binder additions, pelletizing, drying, screening and product packaging. We can also prepare the site, install foundations and provide a building to house the pelletizing system.

The systems can be provided with state-of-the-art electrical control systems including programmable logic controllers for minimum operator attention.

The advantage to you is that your pelletizing system will have a single source responsibility for design and commissioning.

The design of our customized pelletizing systems is a five-step process:

Step 1.

In our laboratory, we determine the pelletizing characteristics of your material and establish equipment requirements for your particular needs.

Step 2.

Using laboratory information, Mars Mineral's engineering staff develops equipment layouts and process instrumentation diagrams for your system.

Step 3.

We select the proper equipment and fabricate it in our own shop and contract with vendors to build your system components to specification.

Step 4.

Mars Mineral delivers the finished equipment to your site, installs foundations and sets the equipment into place.

Step 5.

We provide our system start-up expertise and train your operators.
