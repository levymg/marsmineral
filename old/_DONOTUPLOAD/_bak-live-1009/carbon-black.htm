Pyrolysis Char (a.k.a. Carbon Black)

Let Tire Pyrolysis Char Fuel Your Growth

Now more than ever, it's necessary to find new revenue streams for maintaining profitability. With a Mars Mineral Pelletizing System, you’re able to process tire pyrolysis char and turn it into green.

As a critical pigment and reinforcing agent used in manufacturing automotive, industrial and furniture products, pipe, film and plastics, concrete and mortar, asphalt shingles, adhesives and much more, a pelletizing system helps you:

  • Fill a critical market need
  • Improve your bottom line
  • Make a vital product easier for shippers and manufacturers to handle
  • “Go green” by reducing the environmental impact of a hard-to-handle industrial powder
